Stories, Systems & Mythological Inspiration

I write historical and mythological fiction infused with romance, intrigue, and epic storytelling. Inspired by ancient legends, forgotten histories, and emotionally complex characters, my work explores the delicate line between destiny and desire.

An avid reader of historical fiction and mythology, I am particularly drawn to stories of powerful women, fractured loyalties, and worlds where love and legacy collide. When not writing, I can usually be found buried in books, researching ancient civilizations, or obsessing over fictional characters far too intensely.

I am also fueled almost exclusively by caramel lattes; except in autumn, when all loyalties shift firmly toward pumpkin spice..
